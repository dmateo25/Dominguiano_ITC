CANADA

Banff National Park

Mention Canada tourist spots, for sure, Banff National Park is on top of mind because why not? This Alberta tourist spot boasts turquoise-colored lakes, spectacular glaciers, snow-capped peaks, and undeniably stunning landscapes. Among the most famous places of Canada to check out at Banff National Park are Lake Louise and Moraine Lake.

Niagara Falls

No doubt, Niagara Falls is one of the most famous places of Canada where millions of tourists visit it every year. These iconic waterfalls stand at 57 meters giving a magical experience to those who see it up close.

Whistler

If you’re looking for the best tourist spot in Canada for winter holidays, there’s no place you should rather be than in Whistler. Home to one of the largest ski resorts in North America, Whistler offers some of the most thrilling activities like skiing, snowshoeing, and tobogganing.
